TCHANA PIERRE

PAINTINGS SCULPTURE VISUAL MEDIA NOTES

Tchana Pierre is a Raleigh based artist whose work delves into the physical and psychological effects of human emotions on the mind and body. Through his art, Pierre explores how feelings such as love, joy, anxiety, grief and lust manifest within us shaping our perceptions, behaviors and physical forms. Working primarily in acrylic and film, Pierre combines figuration and abstraction to challenge the viewer's understanding of the real and pseudo-real.
